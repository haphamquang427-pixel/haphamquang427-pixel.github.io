The Brown Bear

The Brown Bear (Ursus arctos) is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America. It is known for its strength and adaptability.

Brown Bear Characteristics

  • Thick fur coat
  • Large body size
  • Omnivorous diet
  • Excellent swimmers

Subspecies (Ordered List)

  1. Eurasian Brown Bear
  2. Grizzly Bear
  3. Kodiak Bear
  4. Syrian Brown Bear

Brown bears play an important role in ecosystems and have cultural significance in many regions.
